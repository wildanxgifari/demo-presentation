Halal Card for Restaurant

Use this card when you go to restaurant/ food stall/ etc

For item in konbini/ supermarket please use halal japan app

Choose the menu/ item you want to order

while pointing at the item/ menu, show the following questions (swipe right)

すみません、これは動物由来のものが入っていますか?

特に気になるのはショートニングと乳化剤です。(動物の油脂を使うことがあるため)

なお、卵や牛乳、また魚介のもは問題ありません。

for English meaning, please swipe down

Excuse me, does this item contain anything animal based?

Especially I’m concerned about shortening and emulsifier, cuz they can contain animal fat.

However eggs, milk and seafood are all OK

for Japanese, please swipe up

please wait for the sales to check, you can confirm with asking

kore wa daijyobu?

this item is OK or not OK?

if the seller says wakaranai (I am not sure), please don't eat it
